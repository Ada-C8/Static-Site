Past

Though born in Miami, I now call the beautiful mountains and water bodies of the Pacific Northwest home. Daughter of immigrant parents, I grew up with three cultures: Cuban, Spanish, and American. Always curious, I drove my mother crazy asking her questions at every turn. That curiosity is now being funneled into the complex, every changing world of software development.

Present

I decided to leave the life I had built in Hood River working as a community health worker and engaging in public health. I was fortunate enough to get into Cohort 8 of Ada Developer's Academy. The journey is just beginning. In 6 short months, we will be at an internship, contributing to increasing diversity in the tech world and getting to work on real life problems.

Future

I hope to contrinute to user experience and research work at a company, increasing diverse perspectives and seeking insight from communities that have traditionally been ignored by the tech world. I want to reimagine the way we interact with customers and users, creating a compassionate and relationship driven connection.
