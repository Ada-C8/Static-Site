ABOUT

Holding a Bachelors Degree in Japanese Linguistics, Isaac has now turned to learning programming languages as their new goal.

Isaac is a current student of Cohort 8 at Ada Developers Academy where they learn about Ruby, HTML/CSS, Rails, Javascript, code time/space complexity, and practices such as refactoring code, Pair Programming, Test Driven Develpment, and Agile methodology.

Before their venture into programming, Isaac worked as a Web Design Specialist for CDK Global building websites using a proprietary CMS system in 2016. During their undergraduate years, they were a Wordpress Web Designer and Graphic Artist for the University of Washington's LGBTQ resource center, the Q Center.

Isaac values a global mindset and diversity's key in creating diverse solutions to any large-scale problem. Isaac studied in Japan at Kyushu University under the Asia In Today's World Program in 2015 where they broadened their knowledge of Japanese society and culture under esteemed staff and faculty.

Isaac speaks intermediate-level Japanese and is a native speaker of Tagalog.

In true Seattle fashion, Isaac calls an overcast sky in the lower 70's a perfect day. They love ube cheesecakes, dogs, and taiko (Japanese drumming).
