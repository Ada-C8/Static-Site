About Me - Work in Progress

My name is Stefanie (I surmised you've figured this out already), but I tend to go by Stef preferably and never Steffie or however you spell it. I hate talking about myself in the third person because I find it to be ridiculous. Thus you get this version. Currently I am a software development student at Ada Developers Academy in Seattle. I live downtown in an eclectic apartment that has a great view of the sound, with my cat Hedwig, my bettas Miso and Swedish, and a lot of plants. My free time is generally spent trying to complete homework and eating. My favourite colour is green and I have a billion other hobbies that I shall talk about some other time.
