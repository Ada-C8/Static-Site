A (half)Peruvian living in Seattle

Or, where did all my melanin go?

I'm an art history nerd who decided I enjoyed looking at art more than I did writing about it (Dissertation: 1, Isabel: 0), so I moved to Seattle to learn web development at Ada Developers Academy.

My two great loves are Legos and Star Wars (yes, I am eyeballing that 7,500-piece Lego Millenium Falcon). Also: parentheticals. If I were a color, it would be orange.

When I'm not longingly staring at Lego sets, I can also be found doing New York Times crossword puzzles.
